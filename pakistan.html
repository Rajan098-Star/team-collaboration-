Most demanded cars in Russia

Koenigsegg

Koenigsegg is a Swedish manufacturer known for producing high-performance hypercars with cutting-edge technology and innovative engineering. The company is renowned for its exceptional speed, exclusive designs, and groundbreaking achievements in automotive performance.

Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is a luxury SUV produced by the Italian manufacturer Lamborghini. Combining the performance of a sports car with the versatility of an SUV, the Urus is designed to offer both extreme power and comfort. It is known for its bold design and advanced technology.

McLaren

McLaren Automotive is a British manufacturer of luxury, high-performance sports cars. The company is known for its success in motorsports, especially Formula 1, and produces road cars that incorporate advanced aerodynamics, lightweight construction, and powerful engines.
